Biography

Abraham Yosief, lives in Toronto. He is a videographer and photographer who studied at Durham College in Oshawa, Ontario. He has experience in capturing photos and videos for those special moments such as weddings, birthdays, graduations, interviews and other religious ceremonies or events.

He also works as an audio-visual and information technology (AV/IT) technician at St. Andrew’s Club and Conference Centre since April, 2012. He operates in different roles such as webcasts, teleconferences, videography and audio technician.

He is deeply interested in photography, videography, video editing and web page designing. Specifically, in cinematography, video editing and image processing are his highlighted areas of expertise.
